DRIVER PORTAL

The driver portal is an exclusive online hub solely accessible to current
employees who are drivers within company. It's their one-stop platform
for scheduling assigned routes and delivery tasks, enabling direct
communication with dispatchers or managers. It streamlines documentation
and reporting, allowing access to invoices and essential records.
Moreover, it facilitates vehicle maintenance tracking, ensuring the fleet
remains in optimal condition. This centralized tool empowers drivers,
providing them with necessary resources to manage tasks efficiently while
maintaining secure, internal communication channels.
