Refrigerated

These trucks are equipped with a refrigeration system to transport
temperature-sensitive goods such as frozen meats, produce,
pharmaceuticals, or chemicals. The trucks are insulated and have a
built-in refrigeration unit that maintains specific temperature
settings, ensuring the cargo remains fresh or at a specified
temperature during transit.

Tanker

Designed to transport liquid or gas cargo in large quantities.
Commonly used for transporting gasoline, chemicals, milk, water,
or other liquids. These trucks have cylindrical tanks securely
mounted on the trailer chassis, ensuring the safe and efficient
transport of various liquid or gas products.

Flatbed

Transports construction materials, machinery, steel beams, or
vehicles. The absence of walls or a roof provides versatility in
loading and securing cargo of various sizes and shapes.
